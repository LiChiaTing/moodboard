Brand Identity & Logo
Three logo directions for the AI Room Transformer product. Each concept works across app bar, favicon, onboarding, and export contexts.
01 — Brand name options
Recommended
Reframe

Why: Dual meaning — "reframe" a room physically + shift your perspective. Clean, memorable, app-nameable.

Option B
Homi

Why: Home + AI. Warm, approachable, short. Feels consumer-first.

Option C
Palette

Why: Evokes design + color curation. Strong visual connotation, easy to brand.

Option D
Stager

Why: Direct, functional. "Stage" your room before buying. Professional ring.

All concepts below use "Reframe" as the working name. Swap easily — the mark stands on its own regardless of wordmark.
