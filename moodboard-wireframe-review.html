Style & Budget

2 critical issues · Redesign needed

🚨 Step 2 has 2 critical issues — must fix before engineering handoff
🚨  Section A (open text box) must be removed. Design principle #1 = no text prompt, ever. Alex can't articulate what they want. Jordan doesn't know what they want. A text box breaks the entire value proposition of this product.
🚨  Style section uses text name tags (Scandinavian, Japandi…) — replace with visual photo tiles. Users shouldn't need to know interior design vocabulary. Show 8–12 real room photos and let them tap what feels right. AI infers the style.
⚠️  Budget must move to the top, before style tiles. Maya has a $120 cap — if she picks style first and then hits budget constraints, she loses trust immediately.
✅  Color mood tiles (Section C) are the right pattern — visual, tappable, no labels required. Apply this same logic to the style section.
✅  Budget slider + AI suggestion ("AI suggests $200–$350") — keep exactly this.
